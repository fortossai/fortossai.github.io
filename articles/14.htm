REGIMENTAL ORDERS.

The following promotions and appointments of non-commissioned officers have been made in the 16th Infantry:

Band,

Private Harry W. Fielding, to be Corporal, vice, Lapine discharged.

Private Herbert E. Roth, to be Corporal, vice, Breen reduced.

Company H,

Corporal Fred Mosely, to be Sergeant, vice, McCrory discharged.

Private William O'Brien, to be Corporal, vice, Mosely promoted.

Company I,

Corporal Locke Castlebury, to be Sergeant, vice, McGinnis discharged.

Lance-Corporal Charles C. Devine, to be Corporal vice, Castlebury promoted.

Private Clyde Voorhees, to be Corporal, vice, Smith reduced.

Company K,

Corporal Lans Nelson, to be Sergeant, vice, Heintz reduced.

Private John G. Minich, to be Corporal, vice, Nelson promoted.
