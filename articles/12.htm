THE HOSPITAL'S DINNER.

Through our special hospital correspondent we have received a humoristic bill of fare of the mess of the Chief Surgeon's office and Medical Supply Depot, consisting of the following members: Harry T. Temple, Anton F. Miller, Robert Anderson and Romain Fair. No wonder that some of them are getting so stout—like democratic aldermen in the States:

BILL OF FARE
NEW YEAR'S DAY

RELISHES

Olives, Radishes, Chutney, Celery, etc.
(on account of rainy season could not be got.)

SOUPS

Mock Turtle, Julienne, Cream Tomatoes
(this course was left out on account of a wrong reckoning in the Commissary Department.)
