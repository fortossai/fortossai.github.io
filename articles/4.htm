MAIL.

The S. S. Compania de Filipinas is expected to arrive to-day or to-morrow, with ten sacks of mail. It is not known whether it is U. S. or local mail.

The Manila "Freedom" states that the "Thomas" is due to arrive in Manila, to-day.

Mail will close, for Manila and the States, about Monday.
