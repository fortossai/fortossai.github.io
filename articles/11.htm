WANTED.

Copies of the following numbers of the News are very much desired in this office: 4, 6, 9, 11, 13 and 14. Any one having any of the above copies they desire to dispose of will confer a favor upon the manager of this paper by sending such copies to this office. The regular price of five cents a copy will be paid for them.
